TinniTuneout

Tune Out Tinnitus

TinniTuneout is a thoughtfully designed tool, created by someone who truly understands what living with tinnitus feels like.

More Than Just an App

It’s a personalized sound companion that helps retrain your brain to pay less attention to tinnitus.

By generating gentle, carefully crafted sounds that steer clear of your tinnitus frequencies, TinniTuneout guides your mind toward more neutral listening experiences. Over time, this can support your brain’s natural ability to tune out the intrusive sounds of tinnitus — a process known as habituation.

Tailored Support for Your Day

Flexible and easy to use, TinniTuneout can be customized to match your unique experience of tinnitus. Used regularly, it can help you create calmer moments — whether to start your morning with less stress, ease through midday spikes, or wind down before sleep.
