Find calm beyond the noise of tinnitus

TinniTuneout is your personalized sound companion, designed to help reduce the impact of tinnitus on your daily life. By generating gentle, frequency‑aware audio, TinniTuneout encourages relaxation and may help shift your attention away from intrusive sounds — a process often referred to as habituation. Whether you’re easing into the day, navigating midday spikes, or winding down before sleep, TinniTuneout helps create calmer moments throughout your routine.

How TinniTuneout helps you

  • Custom sound options: Choose between two unique sound types that aim to reduce tinnitus distractions:
    • Pips — Natural‑sounding tonal pulses generated at frequencies that exclude your tinnitus range. These gentle “pips” resemble soothing natural sounds, like crickets on a warm night.
    • Narrow‑Band Noise — Carefully shaped noise that omits your tinnitus frequencies, helping your mind focus on more neutral audio.
  • Wave‑like patterns: Sounds ebb and flow like ocean waves, gently rising and fading to keep your attention engaged without overwhelming it.
  • Tailor it to your needs: On the Wave tab, adjust how the sounds move — set wave duration and gaps to customize how each wave flows in and out.
  • Timer included: Set a session length that suits your needs, then relax knowing it will stop automatically.
  • Runs in the background: Continue using your phone while your personalized sound environment plays.

Access and subscription

TinniTuneout requires a subscription to access its features. A free 7‑day trial is available to help you explore the app before committing. At the end of the free trial, you will be presented with the option to purchase a monthly subscription to continue using the app. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store account settings.

Privacy‑first design

TinniTuneout values your trust. No accounts or sign‑ups are required. Your tinnitus preferences stay on your device and are never sent to external servers.

Start your journey toward calmer listening

TinniTuneout is not a medical device and does not diagnose or treat tinnitus. It’s designed to support relaxation and sound‑based coping strategies. Regular use may help your brain become less reactive to tinnitus over time, freeing up your focus for the moments that matter most.
