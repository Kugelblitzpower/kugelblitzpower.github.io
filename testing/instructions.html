Reviewer Instructions (App Access Declaration)

  • On first launch, you will be taken to the Info Tab and presented with the Terms and Conditions. Please click “I Accept.”
  • Tap on any of the other tabs.
  • You will then be taken to the Start Free Trial page.
  • Click “Start Free Trial.”
  • Two sign‑in options are available:
    • Sign in with Google – any Google account can be used. This grants a free 7‑day trial with full access.
    • Send Magic Link – enter any valid email address. A magic link will be sent to that email. Clicking the link grants a free 7‑day trial with full access.

All new users automatically receive the free 7‑day trial, so no payment details are required for review.
